Traffic Rules

  • Always wear a helmet while riding two-wheelers.
  • Use seat belts at all times in four-wheelers.
  • Obey traffic signals and signs.
  • Do not use mobile phones while driving.
  • Maintain a safe distance from other vehicles.

Traffic Challans by State

  • Karnataka: ₹300 for not wearing a helmet.
  • Maharashtra: ₹1000 for jumping a red light.
  • Delhi: ₹500 for using a mobile phone while driving.
