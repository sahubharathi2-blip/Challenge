Essential Traffic Rules

  • Always wear a helmet on two-wheelers.
  • Use seat belts inside four-wheelers at all times.
  • Obey traffic signals and signs strictly.
  • Avoid using mobile phones while driving.
  • Maintain a safe distance from other vehicles.

State-wise Traffic Challans

  • Andhra Pradesh – ₹500 for overspeeding (Effective from 01-Oct-2025)
  • Karnataka – ₹300 for no helmet (Effective from 15-Sep-2025)
  • Maharashtra – ₹1000 for red light jump (Effective from 20-Sep-2025)
  • Delhi – ₹500 for mobile phone use while driving (Effective from 10-Oct-2025)
